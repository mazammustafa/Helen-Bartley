ABOUT US

Helen is a Palmer/Pletsch Certified Sewing Instructor, workshop teacher, and author of the Palmer/Pletsch book Fit & Sew Custom Jeans. With unbridled passion and contagious enthusiasm comes a desire to share—and this is what Helen Bartley brings first and foremost to her sewing lounge. It’s reflected in what and how she teaches, her deep dive into details and descriptions, and the way she interacts with students of all sewing experience levels.

Seam Divas beamed brightly into existence in 2008, and Helen has now made it her full-time pursuit, having jettisoned traditional office life in 2016. She’s taught hundreds of students, and has also shared her craft and inspired with her knowledge at numerous workshops and classes across the Pacific Northwest and beyond.

THE LOUNGE

A GREAT PLACE to Create

Seam Divas Sewing Lounge is a fully equipped sewing studio complete with both vintage mechanical and cutting-edge computerized sewing machines, sergers and a cover stitch machine.

You’ll find ample cutting and pressing space, and plentiful book and DVD resources. Whether you’re an experienced sewist or a beginner just dabbling in the art, we think you’ll appreciate the thoughtful setup and quality tools at your disposal.

We’re situated at the top of a long, steep driveway shared with one other home. The address is on the deck.

Parking for Private Lessons: When you arrive for your lesson, please drive to the top and park in front of the garage on the left-hand side.

Parking for Group Lessons: Please park on the street at the end of the drive. If you have difficulty walking, feel free to park in front of the garage.

No need to knock or ring the bell—just come on in!
